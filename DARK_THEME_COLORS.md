# Dark Theme Renkleri

## Ana Arkaplan Renkleri
- `#0F172A` (slate-900) - HTML/Body arkaplan rengi
- `dark:from-gray-800` - Gradient başlangıç rengi
- `dark:via-gray-900` - Gradient orta rengi
- `dark:to-black` - Gradient bitiş rengi

## Navbar Renkleri
- `dark:bg-gray-800/90` - Navbar arkaplan (90% opacity)
- `dark:border-gray-700/50` - Navbar border (50% opacity)
- `dark:text-gray-300` - Navbar metin rengi
- `dark:text-white` - Navbar hover metin rengi
- `dark:hover:bg-gray-700/50` - Navbar hover arkaplan (50% opacity)
- `dark:bg-gray-800/95` - Mobile menu arkaplan (95% opacity)

## Section Arkaplan Renkleri
- `dark:bg-gray-50` - Projects section arkaplan
- `dark:bg-gray-900` - Projects section arkaplan
- `dark:from-gray-800 dark:via-gray-900 dark:to-black` - Hero, About, Technologies, Contact section gradient

## Card Renkleri
- `dark:bg-gray-800/50` - Card arkaplan (50% opacity)
- `dark:bg-gray-800/90` - Card arkaplan (90% opacity)
- `dark:border-gray-700/50` - Card border (50% opacity)
- `dark:bg-gray-700/50` - Card hover arkaplan (50% opacity)

## Metin Renkleri
